About Us

Cadre & Consilium Group, LLC is a Connecticut-based general contracting firm specializing in residential remodeling, structural renovations, and complete home transformations. Combining traditional craftsmanship with modern workflow automation, we deliver projects that meet strict state building codes, safety standards, and compliance requirements while maintaining the highest level of professional integrity.

Our history began in 2018 when we recognized a gap in the market for premium quality remodeling services that balanced modern efficiency with timeless design. Since then, we've completed over 150 projects across Hartford County, earning a reputation for precision, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

• Full-service general contracting
• Structural and architectural renovations
• Custom home additions
• Interior/exterior finishes

We work closely with subcontractors and trade partners to ensure every project is completed on time, within budget, and to our exacting standards. Our team includes licensed contractors, certified architects, and experienced project managers dedicated to excellence in every phase of the remodeling process.
